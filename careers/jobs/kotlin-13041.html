Kotlin Lead Developer Job Description

We are seeking an exceptional Kotlin Lead Developer to lead our talented development team. As a Kotlin Lead Developer, you will play a pivotal role in driving the design, development, and delivery of high-quality mobile and web applications using Kotlin. If you have a passion for Kotlin, possess strong leadership skills, and are ready to take on a leadership role in the world of software development, we want to hear from you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead and mentor a team of Kotlin Developers, providing technical guidance and support.
  • Design and develop complex mobile and web applications using Kotlin.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define project requirements and strategies.
  • Architect scalable and maintainable software solutions.
  • Ensure code quality, maintainability, and adherence to best practices.
  • Optimize applications for performance, security, and user experience.
  • Lead code reviews and provide constructive feedback to team members.
  • Stay updated with Kotlin developments and industry trends.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Proven experience as a Kotlin Developer with a strong portfolio of projects.
  • Demonstrated leadership experience in leading software development teams.
  • Expertise in Kotlin programming and modern mobile/web development frameworks.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to foster collaboration and teamwork within the development team.
