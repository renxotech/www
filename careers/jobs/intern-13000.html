Software Developer Job Description

RENXO Technologies is a growing startup at the cutting edge of technology. We're passionate about innovation and creating solutions that make a real difference. As we continue to grow, we're looking for enthusiastic software developers who are eager to learn and contribute to our backend and mobile development teams. Whether your skills are rooted in Golang or Python for backend development, or Kotlin or Flutter for Android frontend development, we want you on our team!.

Roles and Responsibilities

For Backend Developer (Python/Golang):
  • Assist in the design and development of backend services and APIs.
  • Work under the guidance of senior developers to integrate backend with frontend services.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
  • Learn and apply best practices for efficient and scalable code.
  • Optimize applications for performance, security, and user experience.
  • Stay updated with microservices development and industry trends.
For Android Frontend Developer (Kotlin/Flutter):
  • Assist in the design and development of Android applications.
  • Collaborate with the design team to translate designs and wireframes into high-quality code.
  • Learn and apply new technologies and methodologies to improve application responsiveness and performance.
  • Optimize applications for performance, security, and user experience.
  • Learn under the guidance of senior developers.
  • Stay updated with Android development and industry trends.
