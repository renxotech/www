Embedded and IoT Developer

We are seeking a skilled and passionate ESP32 and Arduino Developer to join our team. As an ESP32 and Arduino Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing embedded systems and IoT solutions using ESP32 and Arduino platforms. If you have a strong background in firmware development, experience with ESP32 and Arduino, and a desire to work on cutting-edge projects, we want to hear from you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and develop embedded systems and IoT solutions using ESP32 and Arduino platforms.
  • Program microcontrollers, sensors, and actuators for various applications.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define project requirements and specifications.
  • Create and maintain firmware for custom hardware devices.
  • Debug and troubleshoot hardware and firmware issues.
  • Develop and optimize communication protocols for IoT applications.
  • Integrate IoT devices with cloud platforms for data collection and analysis.
  • Ensure the reliability, security, and efficiency of embedded systems.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest developments in embedded technologies.
  • Document firmware design, development, and testing processes.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or related field.
  • Proven experience as an ESP32 and Arduino Developer or similar role.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as C/C++ for embedded systems.
  • Strong knowledge of ESP32 and Arduino platforms, including IDEs and libraries.
  • Experience with hardware design and schematic capture is a plus.
  • Familiarity with communication protocols (e.g., UART, SPI, I2C, MQTT).
  • Ability to work with various sensors, displays, and peripherals.
  • Strong problem-solving and debugging skills.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
  • Self-motivated with a passion for learning and staying updated on technology trends.

Additional Skills (Preferred but Not Required)

    Experience with wireless communication technologies (e.g., Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LoRa).
  • Knowledge of real-time operating systems (RTOS) for embedded systems.
  • Previous work on IoT projects and familiarity with IoT platforms.
  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git).
  • Experience with PCB design and layout.
