AI/ML Engineer (Computer Vision) Job Description

Location: Karnal, Haryana
Employment Type: Full-Time

At RENXO Technologies, we are paving the way for innovations in Robotics and Supply Chain. We are seeking a dynamic and motivated AI/ML Engineer with expertise in computer vision to join our team. This role is ideal for someone with a fresh perspective or 1-2 years of experience in the field, though candidates with more experience are also encouraged to apply.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and deploy machine learning algorithms and computer vision models to solve practical problems in the industry.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including data scientists, software engineers, and product managers to integrate AI solutions into our products.
  • Analyze and improve the efficiency, scalability, and stability of our existing machine learning systems.
  • Stay updated with the latest developments in machine learning, artificial intelligence, and computer vision; apply knowledge to enhance project outcomes.
  • Test and validate predictive models and algorithms against real-world data.
  • Document and present model development processes, results, and business impacts to stakeholders.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, or a related field.
  • 1-2 years of experience in developing machine learning and computer vision applications (fresh graduates with relevant project or internship experience may also apply).
  • Strong programming skills in Python and familiarity with machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, Keras, or PyTorch.
  • Experience with image and video processing algorithms.
  • Knowledge of modern deep learning techniques, especially in areas related to computer vision such as CNNs, RNNs, and reinforcement learning.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work in a team environment.
  • Strong communication skills, with the ability to translate technical concepts into non-technical terms.

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
  • Publication in relevant conferences or journals.
  • Contribution to open-source projects related to AI/ML.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and compensation package.
  • Opportunities for professional development and continuing education.
